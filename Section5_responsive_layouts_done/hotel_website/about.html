About Kunyit 7 Lodge

Set in Brunei Water Village, Kunyit 7 Lodge features a terrace and views of the city. Our home is built on a site which belongs to the family since 1926. We have been open to visitors since 2015 and have visitors from more than 48 countries.

All rooms come with a shared bathroom equipped with a shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Kunyit 7 Lodge features free WiFi.

Yayasan Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah Shopping Complex is 500 m from Kunyit 7 Lodge, while Sultan Omar Ali Saifuddien Mosque is 701 m away. Brunei Airport is 4.3 mi from the property.
