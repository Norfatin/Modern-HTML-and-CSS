About Our Lodge

History Of Our Homestay

Kunyit 7 Lodge is located at Jetty 2, Kampong Bakut Berumput. We are a home built on stilts and in the Water Village or Kampong Ayer. Our lodge is a boat ride away from the Water Village cultural and museum gallery. You may sip your morning coffee, watch the children go to school and the fishermen return from sea from the boardwalk of our terrace. Immerse in the sunset as the evening call of prayers as you sit at our pantaran.
